# Calmm 🐾

Welcome to **NAMETOBEDECIDED**, your virtual kitten companion! This project is a simple, engaging **Tamagotchi-style game** where you raise, care for, and train an adorable pixelated (or illustrated) cat.

## 🌟 Project Purpose

Calmm is a **passion project** created with the primary goal of learning and practicing **game development**. It's a playground for exploring core game concepts, including:

* Character state management (hunger, happiness, energy)
* User interface (UI) design for games
* Basic 2D animation techniques
* Networking and server-side logic for real-time interaction

## 🎮 Key Features

Here’s what you can expect to do in Calmm:

### 1. **Raise Your Kitten (Tamagotchi Style)** 🐟
Your kitten needs constant love and attention! Make sure to:
* **Feed It:** Keep its tummy full with different types of fish and treats.
* **Play With It:** Increase happiness through interactive play.
* **Clean It:** Maintain hygiene to keep your pet healthy.
* **Watch It Grow:** Your kitten will evolve as it levels up, unlocking new visuals and abilities.

### 2. **Kitten Minigames** 🕹️
Engage in fun, quick minigames to earn currency and experience points (XP) for your cat. These games challenge your reflexes and strategic thinking.

### 3. **Online Battle System** ⚔️
Once your kitten is strong enough, take the fight online! Challenge friends or random opponents to quick, turn-based "Kitten Battles." Test your training and strategy against other virtual pet owners.
